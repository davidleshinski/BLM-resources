OUR GOAL

It is our hope that we can gather and link essential resources to help individuals who are white and non-Black people of color to educate themselves. Black women, Black men, and Black children face racism every single day throughout the world. We will link resources from books to movies and podcasts, among others to help educate allies. To be a person who is actively anti-racist, you MUST be willing to put in time and/or your money. It is no longer enough to just "not be racist".
